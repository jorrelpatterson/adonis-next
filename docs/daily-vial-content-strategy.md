## Account Rules (Non-Negotiable)
1. Scientific compound class names only - "GLP-1 Receptor Agonist", not "weight loss peptide"
2. No outcome claims - describe what research examined, never what the compound does
3. Disclaimer on every post - short and consistent
4. Elysian visual identity on every post - dark navy, holographic, JetBrains Mono for data labels
5. Never tag products directly - education first, brand association is ambient
