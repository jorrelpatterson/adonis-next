# Ambassador Recruitment Script

This is your copy-paste toolkit for recruiting personal contacts as advnce labs ambassadors over WhatsApp or SMS. Use the version that fits the context — short for a quick ping to someone you already talk to regularly, medium when you want to give them enough to say yes on the spot, long when you're pitching someone who's going to ask questions before they commit.

Variables to fill in before sending:
- `{NAME}` — their first name
- `{HOW_YOU_KNOW_THEM}` — one quick phrase ("you've been asking me about my protocol", "we've trained together forever", "you mentioned you were curious about peptides")
- `{REFERRAL_CODE}` — their ambassador code (all caps, e.g. `EZEKIELPHOTOGRAPHY`)
- `{DASHBOARD_LINK}` — their dashboard URL, e.g. `https://advncelabs.com/advnce-dashboard.html?code=EZEKIELPHOTOGRAPHY`

Replace every `{VARIABLE}` before sending. Don't send the curly braces.

---

## Version 1 — Short (1–2 sentences)

> Hey {NAME} — {HOW_YOU_KNOW_THEM}, so I wanted to ask first: want to be an advnce labs ambassador? I'll send you everything you need, takes two minutes to set up, and you earn every time someone orders through your link.

---

## Version 2 — Medium (4–5 sentences)

> Hey {NAME} — {HOW_YOU_KNOW_THEM}, so I figured you'd be a natural fit for this. I'm building out the ambassador program for advnce labs (research-grade peptides — the brand I've been running). The setup is simple: you get a personal referral link, share it when it makes sense, and earn 10% commission on every order. There's no quota, no pressure, no weird MLM stuff — just a real commission on real orders. Interested?

---

## Version 3 — Long (full pitch, handles common questions upfront)

> Hey {NAME} — hope you're good. Quick question for you.
>
> I'm growing the ambassador team for advnce labs and {HOW_YOU_KNOW_THEM} — so you were one of the first people I thought of.
>
> Here's how it works: you get a personal referral link and a set of ready-to-use share images + caption templates (I do all the creative work for you). Anyone who orders through your link earns you a 10% commission on their first order, 15% starting from your 6th referral, and 20% once you've brought in 15+. If any of your referrals bring in someone else, you get 5% on that second-level order too, and 2.5% on third-level. It compounds.
>
> You don't need to post on a schedule or hit any minimums. If it fits a conversation you're already having, share the link. That's it.
>
> Payouts go out monthly via Zelle/Venmo — no minimum threshold.
>
> The product sells itself to people who care about sourcing: every compound ships with manufacturer COAs and HPLC verification. No mystery powder stuff. People who research this space know the difference.
>
> If you're in, I'll send you your personal link, a set of share images, and three caption templates — all ready to go in about five minutes.
>
> Want in?

> Let's go, {NAME} — you're in. Here's everything you need:
>
> **Your referral link:**
> https://advncelabs.com?ref={REFERRAL_CODE}
>
> **Your asset kit** (share images + caption templates + FAQ):
> https://advncelabs.com/advnce-asset-kit.html?code={REFERRAL_CODE}
>
> **Your dashboard** (track earnings, referrals, payout history):
> {DASHBOARD_LINK}
>
> The asset kit has two Story-format images, two Feed-format images, and three caption templates ready to copy. Everything already has your code baked in.
>
> Payouts go out the first week of each month for the prior month's orders. Questions, just hit me back.

## 1-Week Check-In — After Onboarding

Send this roughly 7 days after their onboarding message, whether or not they've shared anything yet.

> Hey {NAME} — checking in on the advnce labs ambassador thing. Any questions come up? Happy to hop on a call for 10 minutes if it's easier.
>
> Your link is still: https://advncelabs.com?ref={REFERRAL_CODE}
>
> And your asset kit if you need a refresher on the images/captions: https://advncelabs.com/advnce-asset-kit.html?code={REFERRAL_CODE}
>
> No pressure at all — just wanted to make sure you have what you need. Let me know.
